# Support

Need help with Scorpio? Here are some ways to get support:

## 🐛 Report a Bug
If you've found a bug, please [open an issue](https://github.com/RushilMahadevu/scorpio/issues/new) on GitHub.

## 💡 Suggest a Feature
Have an idea for a new feature? We'd love to hear it! Please [open an issue](https://github.com/RushilMahadevu/scorpio/issues/new) on GitHub.

## 📧 Email Support
For account-specific issues or direct queries, you can reach out to us at **rushil@scorpioedu.org**.

## 📖 Documentation
Check out the `src/app/(docs)` folder for information about Scorpio's vision, research, privacy policy, and terms of service.
